title: Icy Caves

participants: ["Kalilia Crowdance", "Alsol", "Lydia Naïkiir"]

Kalilia, Alsol and Lydia are at the bottom of an icy cave, in which they climbed down to in the last session. Kalilia rolls a nat 20 strength check to retrieve her rope. With the nat 20, she manages to retrieve both ropes.

They make their way through the cave. Kalilia finds two notes not too far away from the entrance that read:
> *We have begun our search for the Lost Laboratory! Legend say there is a type of armour there that makes the wearer much stronger. I have good faith in this group. We are many, we have a lot of gear and the morale is on top. We are ready to face the challenges of this cave!*

> *I cannot stress how grateful I am for the detailed map we got. The Cartophile truly did an amazing job!*

They continue on through the caves and soon enough they come across a pretty big room, in which two frost giants are roaming. They carefully peek into the room to be able to make a plan: to lure the frost giants into the narrow corridor so they can fight them one at a time.

They decide to follow through with their plan, and attack the frost giants. They're surprised!

Frost giant 2 tries to throw rocks at the party, but rolls a nat 1 twice and hits frost giant 1 in the head.

Kalilia kills frost giant 1, but Alsol later gets downed by frost giant 2. So does Kalilia. Fortunately enough, Lydia manages to kill it and they all live to fight another day.

A long fight requires a long rest, so they sleep.
