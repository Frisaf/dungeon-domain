---
title: The Ravine
date: 2026-04-19
participants: ["Kalilia Crowdance", "Alsol", "Lydia Naïkiir"]
rewards: ["Level up to level 5!"]
---
Our heroes wake up from a long rest in the ice cave. They continue on, and discover a new room filled with a swarm of bats. Lydia tries to sneak past them, but fails. The bats, now awoken from their slumber, attacks her. The others join in. The bats are, however, no match for the party, and they kill them with ease.

Alsol wants to skin the bats so that she can potentially sell the skins in the future. Kalilia helps her. Alsol suggests skinning the Frost Giants as well, but Kalilia tells her it's not worth it.

In the next room, they encounter a ravine. The floor is very slippery, so everyone has to roll a dexterity saving throw. Lydia rolls a nat 20, and saves herself with a backflip. Kalilia on the other hand, rolls a nat 1, slips and takes 2 bludgeoning damage. Alsol rolls a 5 and slips. She struggles to stand up again.

They discuss how they should cross the ravine. Lydia wants to jump across, so they tie a rope around her waist, and then she jumps. Her fingertips grace the other side of the ravine, but close is not enough - she fails. Kalilia pulls her up with the rope.

They decide to discover another room instead. Once there, they recognize the equipment spread out on the ground from the beginning of the cave. Among all of the items, they find another note that reads:
>*Day 4: Things aren't looking great. It was much colder than we had anticipated, and we are almost out of food. We have found an irregular statue that we think might have something to do with the Lost Laboratory. It had a key on an altar that we took*

They walk back to the ravine and try to bridge the gap with a ladder they found. Lydia tries to jump across again, but fails. Alsol tries as well, and manages to grab the ledge of the other side. She rolls a nat 1 and falls down.

On Alsol's second attempt, she makes it. She digs a pickaxe she found into the ground. This makes the icy ground crack up a little.

Lydia also makes it across by climbing on the rope between Alsol and Kalilia. The latter gets dragged up by Alsol and Lydia because she didn't make the jump.

They continue to the next room, in which there is a creature. As they walk closer, the creature retreats into a door which closes and locks behind it.

Since this seems to be a dead end, our friends decide to go back. They cross the ravine for the second time and mines their way into a broken off path they saw before. Once in there, they find a treasure chest filled with 300 GP and a ring:

**Lucky Ring: protects the wearer (and the wearer only!) from bad luck. If there is no source of bad luck close by, the wearer receives the lucky feat.**

They split the gold amongst themselves (100 GP each), and Lydia takes the ring. She also finds another note, along with a key. The note reads:
> *Day 6: Our food has run out, and we are stuck in a cave. The roof collapsed behind us when we entered. There is a chest in here filled with treasure, but how will that help us? We cannot eat gold. To whoever reads this, please do not pick up the key.*

The party takes another long rest, during which Lydia tries to figure out what properties the ring has. Kalilia makes Alsol and Lydia promise to give her wife 500 GP in the case that she would die. Lydia gives the ring to Kalilia because she has already touched the key - they think the key has got unlucky properties.

They make their way across the ravine once again, and unlocks the door with the key. It fits perfectly in the keyhole and the door opens. Kalilia gives the ring back to Lydia, and Lydia gives the 500 GP back to Kalilia becuase she didn't die.
