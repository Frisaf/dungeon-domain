The party exits the cave where they solved all the puzzles in the last session. Once outside, they come across a river, which they successfully cross. On the other side is a town, or at least what used to be one. It's the ruins of Uraliss, which seems to be the current residence of a few orcs, Kalilia and Lydia discover.

Lydia sneaks closer and tries to listen in to any potential conversation, but there is none. Kalilia walks forward, and an orc screams:
*"INTRUDERS! ATTACK!"*
She convinces them not to, but they still attack. The entire party joins the fight. Despite losing numbers rapidly, they insist they are the "greatest warriors that have ever lived". That's probably the last thing they say before they meet their fate. They decide to spare the last remaining orc, so Lydia casts sleep on them. She then hits the orc to wake them up, but they don't. Lydia then realises that she just has to stop concentrating on the spell for it to lose effect.

The orc wakes up, and they interrogate them. They tell them that the orcs live there. They are rather unhelpful, so they decide to just leave them there and loot the houses instead. They manage to find:
- 20 GP
- Rusty weapons:
    - Shortsword
    - Hand axe
    - Knife
- A letter signed by "The Cartophile"
- An unopened letter. It is still sealed.

The first letter reads:
> *"Dear Callie, I've received your message and I have sent a map to you with instructions on how to reach the Lost Laboratory. Good luck on your adventures! Sincerely, The Cartophile."*

They decide to open the sealed letter. It reads:
>*"Dear Callie, it seems like I sent the wrong map to you the last time. I hope you may receive this letter in time before you head out. Please find me in my home in Nogvurot and give me the map I sent you. I can then give you the correct map. Sincerely, The Cartophile."*

Looking at the map, they discover Nogvurot is south of the Blooming Grove, which is where they are headed. They want to go further south later, down to Port Damali. So they decide to continue their journey to the Blooming Grove and skip acting out the travelling there (time skip essentially).
