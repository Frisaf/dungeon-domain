title: Riddles
date: 2026-04-26
participants: ["Kalilia Crowdance", "Alsol", "Lydia Naïkiir"]
---

They enter the newly unlocked temple.

>*You walk through the narrow, dark stone corridor. You can hear water dripping around you, and feel the moist in the air. You can also feel a faint wind on your skin. It doesn't take long until you start to see the light in the tunnel. You can see the sunlight shining on a thin layer of bright, white snow. To your left, the dark tunnel continues. When staring down the slightly downwards sloping tunnel, you can see a pair of eyes staring back at you from somewhere within.*

They choose to continue through the dark tunnel, and move towards the glowing eyes. They stare back at the party with eternal wisdom. As they walk further, the room becomes more spacious. They are no longer standing in the same narrow corridor as before, but a large room. Suddenly, the room gets lit up by green torches sitting high up on the wall. A pair of stone eyes decorate the wall in front of them, and below it, is an entrance to another room.

They try to move towards the entrance, unsuccessfully. It feels as if though the room is endlessly long, and when they stop moving, they are still in the same place as before.

Lydia makes an Arcana check. There is magic all around them in the room, so it doesn't really help. She tries to shoot one of the eyes with an arrow, but it just bounces off the wall.

Kalilia tries to close her eyes and move towards the other side of the room. She is successful, and soon enough, the other two have also crossed to the other side.

The next room is about the same size, but this one has a balcony where the eyes were in the previous room. The mysterious creature is standing on it, staring down at the party. As they enter, it says:
> *"Well done, mortals. You completed the first room. Will your wisdom be enough to challenge mine though? Answer these riddles three and you may continue on your quest to find the long lost adventurer's treasures."*

The first riddle reads:
> *"No man can kill me, try as they might*

> *Though I can’t speak, I can still bite*

> *I come about in the shortest of days,*

> *Protected from the light’s cruel rays."*

They have 3 guesses for each riddle. Lydia guesses "frost", and it's correct.

The second riddle reads:
> *"Patient and kind, a concept of old*

> *I’m all a man wants, worth more than gold*

> *As much of a boon as I am a vice*

> *You can’t buy me for I haven’t a price"*

1. Kalilia guesses "time", but it's incorrect.
2. Lydia guesses "love", and it's correct

The third riddle reads:
> *"Light as a feather, or heavy as lead*

> *The state of my being, controlled in your head*

> *Forever with you, though I can be shared*

> *If I am broken, I can’t be repaired."*

They take the riddle for this one. The riddle is blood spreading out on the floor.

1. Kalilia guesses "life", but it's incorrect.
2. Lydia guesses "promise", but it's incorrect.
3. Lydia guesses "heart", and it's correct.

They can finally move on to the next room, which is another puzzle. There is a wooden door at the opposite side of the room, and it has a riddle inscribed on it:
> *“The key is found within you, use the key to paint me red, be aware of what you spent, for it might be the very end”.*

Kalilia takes her sword and cuts herself. She smears the blood on the door, but with no result. She tries to paint the words red, but nothing happens. She tries to draw a key with blood on the door. A click can be hear, and the door is unlocked.

The creature congratulates them, once they enter the next room, in which they find a whole set of armour, which is cursed.

Both Kalilia and Alsol tries to put it on, but they become possessed. It feels like as if their body is no longer their own. The others have to drag them out for them to stop being possessed.

During a long rest, Lydia decides to study the armour.
