The party has been banished from Uthodurn and get transported back to the city gates with the same escord that brought them to the Grandcast Citadel. Once there they get all their belongings back. They are then let out into the mountains.

Once outside of the city walls, you can feel the biting cold of the Flotket Alps. Although it's cold, you can't help but be amazed by the beautiful landscape; the snow covered ground glitters in the sun - which you have not seen in several days, the pine trees stand tall and proud in front of you and all around you, and the rough slopes around you rise to dizzingly sharp peaks.

Kalilia has still got the warm clothes she bought back in Palebank Village. Alsol has got some as well since she has travelled around for quite some time. Lydia is the only one who hasn't got any. Kalilia gives her cold clothes to Julia because her satyr fur protects her a little bit.

They want to go to Uraliss, and then to Port Damali through the Blooming Grove. They go back and ask a guard for the directions, but he advises them to not go to Uraliss because they are banned from Uthodurn. Since Uraliss is under Uthodurn's control, they wouldn't be welcome there either. He still tells them how to get there though.

So they start to walk in that direction, and after some time they can feel that a blizzard has started to form. Everyone gets -10 in movement speed and are blinded. They continue to move in the same direction, which gives everyone exhaustion level 1. By searching the area, they reach exhaustion level 2. Lydia and Kalilia don't find anything, but Alsol manages to find a crevice in the mountain walls they can seek shelter in.

They all go in, and inside they can see there is a hole in the ground at the very bottom of the cave. Lydia looks into the hole with a lit lantern, but don't see anything of interest.

The party decides that this is a perfect opportunity for a long rest, which reduces their exhaustion level to 1. When they wake up again, the blizzard has not calmed down yet. Instead of risking their lives in the cold, they decide to climb down the mysterious hole instead. Kalilia uses her rope, but it is too short. The hole is approximately 40 meters deep.

To help Kalilia, Lydia throws down a rope to her so she can catch it. She ties the rope to the one she is hanging from, but it is still 9 meters too short. Kalilia decides to risk it for the biscuit and drops down. She takes 7 damange. Alsol climbs down and makes the same jump. She takes 14 damage. Lydia climbs down the rope, and grabs onto the other frozen rope that was there from before. Although it's old, it manages to hold her weight and she can climb down safely.
